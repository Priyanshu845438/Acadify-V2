Onboarding Challenges That Impact Success

Poor onboarding experiences lead to high dropout rates, delayed time-to-value, and reduced satisfaction for both customers and employees.

High Dropout Rates

Complex, lengthy onboarding processes cause 60% of users to abandon before completion, resulting in lost customers and wasted acquisition costs.

  • Complex registration forms
  • Lengthy verification processes
  • Confusing user flows
Delayed Time-to-Value

Manual processes and poor guidance prevent users from experiencing value quickly, leading to reduced engagement and early churn.

  • Slow activation process
  • Lack of guidance
  • Manual approval workflows
Poor User Experience

Inconsistent interfaces, missing information, and lack of personalization create frustrating first impressions that damage long-term relationships.

  • Inconsistent design
  • Generic experiences
  • Lack of progress indicators

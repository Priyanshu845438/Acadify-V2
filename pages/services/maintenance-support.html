Our Incident Response Protocol

A proven process for rapid issue resolution and business continuity.

1. Instant Alert & Triage

Our 24/7 automated monitoring detects an anomaly. An alert is instantly triggered to our on-call engineers in India, who begin immediate triage within 15 minutes.

2. Root Cause Diagnostics

Our team uses advanced diagnostics to pinpoint the exact root cause, moving beyond surface-level symptoms to ensure the problem is fully understood.

3. Resolution & Hotfix

We deploy a fix from our library of solutions or develop a custom hotfix. If necessary, a temporary workaround is implemented to restore service immediately.

4. System Verification

Post-resolution, we conduct rigorous testing to verify that the fix is effective and has not introduced any regressions. Normal performance is confirmed.

5. Post-Mortem & Prevention

We provide a full incident report and update our monitoring and maintenance routines to prevent the issue from reoccurring, strengthening your system over time.
